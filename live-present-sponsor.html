Present or Sponsor a Live Session

Help shape what's next for Dental Wisdom Live

Dental Wisdom Live is our monthly online CE series for the Dental Wisdom network — live, interactive sessions with real-time Q&A and CE credits. We're always glad to hear from dental professionals who'd like to present, and from businesses interested in supporting the program.

Want to Present a Session?

If you're a dental professional or industry expert with knowledge worth sharing, we'd love to hear from you. Live sessions cover clinical techniques, practice management, wellness, and other topics relevant to modern dental practice, followed by real-time Q&A with our community.

Email us a short summary of your proposed topic and we'll be in touch to talk through scheduling.

Want to Sponsor a Session?

Dental Wisdom Live is a great way for businesses to connect with an engaged community of dental professionals. Sponsorship opportunities may include session mentions, giveaways, and more.

Email us to learn about current opportunities.

Get in Touch

Email us at Info@DentalWisdom.org to discuss presenting or sponsoring a Dental Wisdom Live session.
